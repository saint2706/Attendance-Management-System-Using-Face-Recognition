Known Limitations

  • No demographic data: The system does not store sensitive demographic attributes (gender, ethnicity, age). Fairness is measured using proxy groups (role, site, lighting) rather than demographic groups.
  • Small sample sizes: Some groups may have very few samples, making their metrics less reliable. Review the "Samples" column before drawing conclusions.
  • Lighting heuristic: The lighting classification is based on grayscale mean pixel intensity—a coarse proxy that cannot distinguish shadows, glare, or occlusions.
  • Temporal drift: Model performance may degrade over time as cameras change or new conditions arise. Re-run audits periodically.

For a complete discussion of limitations, see the Fairness & Limitations documentation .
